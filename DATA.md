## How was this filtered?

The 374 diseases were curated from the EBI GWAS Catalog (89,981 studies) by:

1. **Keeping only meaningful disease categories** â€” dropping pure quantitative measurements
   (e.g., "blood pressure", "height") that are not diseases per se.
2. **Filtering out under-powered studies** â€” requiring at least 10 GWAS-significant
   associations in the original publication.
3. **Capping at 50 studies per category** so the batch finishes in hours, not days, and
   so each category has comparable representation.

You can re-filter to a wider net (e.g., 200 per category, or all categories) by editing
[scripts/filter_diseases.py](https://huggingface.co/spaces/jianlizhao/GeneDisease-MR-Browser/blob/main/scripts/filter_diseases.py)
and re-running the batch (see [RUN.md Â§B.5](RUN.md#b5-regenerate-the-data-from-scratch-optional)).

## Disease-name to GWAS-ID lookup tips

In the app, the disease dropdown displays:

```
<disease_name>  (ebi-a-GCSTxxxxxx)
```

You can also type partial words â€” the dropdown filters live.
Multi-word searches work too: "heart failure" shrinks the list to just heart-failure studies.

When you pick one, the **disease metadata panel** auto-fills with:
- Total sample size + ancestry breakdown (e.g., "European 100k, East Asian 20k")
- Cases / controls split
- PubMed link to the source paper
- Direct link to the GWAS Catalog page and the OpenGWAS dataset
